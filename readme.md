# Crypto Whitepaper Analyst GPT

## About the Project

Crypto Whitepaper Analyst GPT is an AI-powered research assistant that helps users understand crypto projects without reading long and complex whitepapers.

Simply upload a whitepaper and the GPT generates a structured report covering the project's purpose, technology, tokenomics, risks, competitors, and overall potential.

The goal of this project is to make crypto research easier, faster, and more accessible for beginners, investors, traders, and Web3 enthusiasts.

---

## What It Does

The GPT analyzes crypto whitepapers and provides:

- Easy-to-understand project summaries
- Technology breakdowns
- Tokenomics analysis
- Risk assessments
- Competitor comparisons
- Use case evaluations
- Investment thesis reports
- Overall project scoring

---

## Key Features

### Executive Summary
Quick overview of what the project does and why it exists.

### Tokenomics Analysis
Reviews token utility, supply, allocations, inflation risks, and distribution models.

### Risk Assessment
Identifies potential risks related to technology, adoption, competition, regulation, and tokenomics.

### Competitor Comparison
Compares the project with similar protocols and competitors in the market.

### Beginner-Friendly Explanations
Converts complex blockchain concepts into simple language.

### Project Scoring
Generates a structured score based on multiple evaluation factors.

---

## Why I Built This

While researching crypto projects, I noticed that many whitepapers are difficult to understand, especially for beginners.

I built this GPT to simplify the research process and help users quickly understand a project's strengths, weaknesses, risks, and opportunities without spending hours reading technical documents.
